# Friday: Kettlebell Strength Spec

**Focus:** Posterior chain, core stability, and functional power.
**Total Duration:** 60 Minutes

- **Equipment:** Kettlebells (8, 12, 16, 24 kg), Yoga mat.
- **Rest between sets:** 60-90 seconds.

| **Glute Bridges** | 3    | 15        | Bodyweight       | Hold 2s at top. Squeeze glutes.                                              |
| **Plank**         | 3    | 45-60s    | Bodyweight       | Flat back, engage core.                                                      |
